ABOUT COVID-N-CARE.

The year 2020 has no doubt been a difficult year for many, mostly because of the corona virus pandemic.

loss of job, loss of loved ones to mention a few has largely affected us all emotionally. As we take care of ourselves physically, we need to also care for our mental health. The rate of suicide is on the rise.

Covid-n-Care. is an Non Governmental Organization(NGO) task with the mission of helping to manage people's mental health in Nigeria

It helps bridge the gap between mental health care providers and patients
